radio

Born in 2019, Collect Radio is the experimental outcome of decades & decades of listening, playing, booking and loving music. Curated by musical professionals and artists, with love, open-mindedness, and a need for discovering & promoting good music. Our indoors livestream radio has hosted more than 100 artists and 500 dj sets. Non-discriminatory in nature & utopic in ambition, Collect Radio is both a platform for established international artists and undiscovered and up-and-coming prospects.
