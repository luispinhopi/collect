collect records

First ever release of Collect Records its a reissue of old tracks written and produced by SLOPE aka Daniel Paul & Hans Schaaf between 1999 and 2005. 4 rare Deep House, Future Jazz and Electro gems! Classic stuff! Collect Records is part of the well known COLLECT (Food - Radio - Records) project in Cais do Sodré - Lisboa.
