collect films

Delving into an unexplored field, we went on to start Collect’s film-making project. This project was humbly born in 2021, and has since been growing in both ambition and quality. This project grew from a need to turn some of society’s roughest corners and socially controversial themes into a dialogue. A dialogue that, in turn, looks bring light to peripheral, outcasted & marginalized themes, in video format.
