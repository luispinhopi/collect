about

Originally a Food/Record store, located in Lisbon, with a live music radio, Collect expanded into a Music Label & Musical Event Producer. As of today, Collect is one of Lisbon's booming musical forces, extending from live events/festivals, to producing upcoming electronic artists, to the direct selling of premium vinyl records. Collect has it's HQ in Cais do Sodré, Lisbon, Portugal. Born as a passion product, in 2019, of brothers João Maria Girão, Bernardo Girão & DJ Mariana Barosa. Record Store, Music Festival Organizer, Live Radio, Record Label, Restaurant & Bar -- Collect, has been making itself heard in the portuguese electronic scene.
